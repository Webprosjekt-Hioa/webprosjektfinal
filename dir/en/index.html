Current Situation

Important information regarding school and studies is not easily accessible with the network's current recourses. Many websites are needed to get the information you need and the websites themselves are not universally designed. This means that many students go unaware of important information and the poor design of the websites does not motivate the students to stay updated. And even if they have the motivation, the information will be hard to find.

This is a problem from both a social and technological perspective. Socially, the poorly accessible information creates problems with communication between teacher and student. Any message that the teacher publishes online is hard to see and there is little to no feedback the teacher can receive from the students. This may leave students uninformed about important news forcing them to rely on other students or Facebook groups for information.
From a technological perspective, the interface of the websites creates challenges in the form of accessibility. The main website is not universally designed only having a language option the user can change. Other options like text-size, high contrast and search field are missing. The website does not adapt when you zoom in with your browser and the webpage product is over seven years old.
